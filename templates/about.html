Our Story & Mission

Afaq Academy was established in 2010 with a singular vision: to provide the students of Rahim Yar Khan access to world-class education at their doorstep. Founded by Principal Muhammad Ejaz, the academy began with a small cohort of students and a team of passionate educators.

Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted and respected educational institutions in the region, producing hundreds of board toppers, doctors, engineers, and professionals who are contributing to Pakistan's development.

Under the leadership of Principal Muhammad Ejaz and Vice Principal Khadija Nargis, Afaq Academy continues to innovate and elevate the standard of education in Southern Punjab.

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible education that develops academic excellence, moral character, and critical thinking skills in every student — empowering them to succeed in higher education and beyond.

🌅

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Rahim Yar Khan, recognized for producing the nation's future leaders, scholars, scientists, and professionals with strong Islamic values.

💎

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, compassion, and respect are the cornerstones of Afaq Academy. We believe in treating every student as an individual and nurturing their unique talents and abilities.
